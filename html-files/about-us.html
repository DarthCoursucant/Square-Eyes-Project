What Is Square Eyes ?

Welcome to Square Eyes, your premier destination for movies and cinematic experiences from around the globe!

As a leading global company in the movie retail industry, Square Eyes is your one-stop shop for an extensive selection of movies across all genres and formats.

Whether you're a fan of the latest blockbuster hits, timeless classics, or indie gems, we have something for every movie enthusiast.

With a presence in numerous countries, we pride ourselves on bringing the magic of the silver screen to millions of homes worldwide.

Our mission is to provide unparalleled access to a diverse array of films, ensuring that movie lovers everywhere can indulge in their passion for cinema.

Our Unique Trade-In Service

At Square Eyes, we believe in the power of recycling and the joy of discovery.

That's why we offer a unique trade-in service that allows you to trade your old movie DVDs of any type for a special coupon.

This coupon can be used towards the purchase of new movies from our vast collection.

It's our way of helping you keep your movie library fresh and exciting, while also promoting sustainable practices.

By participating in our trade-in program, you not only get to enjoy new films, but you also contribute to reducing waste and giving your old DVDs a new lease on life.

It's a win-win for both you and the environment!

History of Square Eyes: Celebrating a Legacy of Cinematic Excellence

Established in 2000, Square Eyes was founded by a group of passionate film enthusiasts with a shared vision of making movies accessible to audiences worldwide. What started as a humble storefront selling DVDs in a bustling city has since evolved into a global powerhouse in the movie retail industry, redefining the way people experience and engage with cinema.

Early Years: The Birth of a Cinematic Dream

In its early years, Square Eyes quickly gained a reputation for its curated collection of movies, personalized customer service, and commitment to quality. As the demand for movies grew, so did the company's reach, expanding its presence to new cities and countries, delighting movie lovers everywhere with an unparalleled selection of films from around the world.

Innovation and Expansion: Pioneering the Trade-In Service

In 2010, Square Eyes introduced its groundbreaking trade-in service, a pioneering initiative that revolutionized the way people interacted with their movie collections. By offering customers the opportunity to exchange their old DVDs for new ones through a coupon system, Square Eyes not only promoted sustainability but also fostered a sense of community among movie enthusiasts.

Global Reach: Spreading the Love of Cinema Far and Wide

As the company continued to grow, Square Eyes established a robust online presence, enabling customers from different corners of the globe to access its extensive movie catalog with ease. Today, Square Eyes operates in multiple countries, serving millions of customers and connecting them with the magic of cinema in ways never thought possible.

A Legacy of Excellence: Shaping the Future of Movie Retail

With a legacy built on a passion for film, a commitment to customer satisfaction, and a spirit of innovation, Square Eyes remains at the forefront of the movie retail industry. As we look to the future, we are dedicated to continuing our mission of celebrating the art of cinema, promoting sustainability, and creating memorable experiences for movie lovers worldwide.

Join us on this cinematic journey as we write the next chapter in the history of Square Eyes, where the love for movies knows no bounds and the magic of storytelling lives on.
