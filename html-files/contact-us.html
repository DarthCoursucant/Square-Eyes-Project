Returns and Exchanges

1. Return Eligibility

- You may initiate a return or exchange within 30 days of the purchase date.

- To be eligible for a return, the item must be in its original condition, including all packaging and accessories.

- Please note that digital downloads and streaming services are not eligible for returns.

2. How to Initiate a Return or Exchange:

- To start the return process, please contact our dedicated Customer Service team at our

Email

- Or call us at +1-555-123-4567.

- Provide the team with your order number, details of the item you wish to return, and the reason for the return.

Return Shipping;

- Customers are responsible for the cost of return shipping unless the return is due to an error on our part.

- We recommend using a trackable shipping method to ensure the safe return of the item.

4. Refunds/Exchanges:

- Upon receipt of your returned item, our team will inspect it and notify you of the status of your refund or exchange.

- Refunds will be issued to the original payment method used for the purchase.

- Exchanges will be processed promptly, subject to product availability.
